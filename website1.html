Achievements

Over the years, our Art Club has proudly competed in school-level and national art contests, winning awards for their creativity and collaboration. Our members have been featured in art exhibitions, and our collaborative artworks are currently display at important places across campus. These accomplishments highlight our members' talent, passion, and persistence.
