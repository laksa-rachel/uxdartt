About

The Art Club is a welcoming space for all students to explore and enjoy art. We celebrate creativity and self-expression through hands-on projects and engaging training sessions. More than just a club, we are a creative community where artistic skills grow and friendships form.

What We Do

  • Weekly sketch sessions
  • Mural painting projects
  • Art competitions & exhibitions
  • Collaboration with other CCAs (e.g. props for drama)
  • Community service through art (e.g. recycling art)
